# Code of Conduct

## Enforcement
If you encounter unacceptable behavior or have any concerns, please reach out to the enforcement team via email at enforcement@example.com. We will address your concerns as promptly as possible.

## Response Timelines
We strive to respond to reports within 48 hours, and a full resolution may take up to 14 days. We appreciate your patience during this process.

## Appeal Process
If you feel a decision taken by the enforcement team was unjust or you would like to contest any outcome, you can submit an appeal via email at appeals@example.com within 7 days of the outcome notification.

## Privacy Details
All reports and communications related to enforcement will be handled confidentially. Only the necessary personnel will be involved in the resolution process, and personal information will remain private unless consent is given to disclose it.

## Good Faith Exceptions
We recognize that there may be misunderstandings or miscommunications. If a participant is asked to change their behavior but they can demonstrate that their intentions were not harmful, they may be granted a good faith exception in certain situations.

## Development-Specific Examples
- Using inclusive language and avoiding jargon is crucial in discussions.
- Ensuring that contributions respect the various backgrounds of all participants during code reviews.
- Treating all members of the team with respect during discussions, no matter their experience level.

Thank you for contributing to our community!
